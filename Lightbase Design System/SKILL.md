If the user invokes this skill without any other guidance, ask them what they want to build or design, ask some questions, and act as an expert designer who outputs HTML artifacts _or_ production code, depending on the need.

## Quick orientation
- **Brand:** Lightbase — Montréal architectural lighting (lightbase.ca). Premium, dark, minimal, photographic.
- **Tokens:** `colors_and_type.css` — black/white monochrome + warm-tungsten accent, Inter, 8px spacing, mostly-flat shadows, square or pill radii.
- **Voice:** Confident, understated, "we" pronouns, no emoji, two-beat headlines + one-line subheads.
- **Imagery:** Real photography in `assets/` — products, sectors, and architectural lifestyle.
- **UI kit:** `ui_kits/website/` — full marketing-site recreation with reusable components.
- **Iconography:** Native = 2 chevrons. For UI surfaces, substitute Lucide.
